Drone-Based Data Collections

Drones and remote sensors to advance our understanding of the world.

Aerial Perspectives

CGR is equipped with a variety of drones and remote sensors that can be requested and tasked to collect data in support of investigations conducted by other groups. Available systems include United States federally compliant drones that have been cleared by the Department of Defense (Blue UAS/Blue List).

Check available drones and sensors here and then use this page to provide information on your drone data collection needs and to request information on system availability and collection costs.

RGB Imagery

Geotagged RGB images can be collected and stored in compressed and/or raw format. Image acquisition possibilities include the cameras and drones below:

  • 20 MP camera
    • 4/3 CMOS sensor
    • drone: DJI Mavic 3
  • 20 MP camera
    • 4/3 CMOS sensor
    • RGB + multispectral
    • RTK enabled
    • drone: DJI Mavic 3M
  • 20 MP camera
    • 4/3 CMOS sensor
    • drone: DJI Phantom 4 Pro
  • 48 MP camera
    • RGB images can be collected simultaneous to thermal images
    • drone: Autel EVO II Dual

4-Band Multispectral Imagery

Geotagged multispectral images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drone below:

  • 5 MP multispectral camera
  • spectral regions: green, red, red edge, near infrared
  • images can be collected simultaneous to 20 MP RGB images
  • drone: DJI Mavic 3M

Collections include data from a downwelling radiation sensor and ground calibration panel.

5-Band Multispectral Imagery

Geotagged multispectral images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drone below:

  • Micasense RedEdge MX
    • 1.2 MP multispectral camera
    • spectral regions: blue, green, red, red edge, near infrared
    • drones: Freefly AltaX, DJI Matrice 600 Pro

Collections include data from a downwelling radiation sensor and ground calibration panel.

10-Band Multispectral Imagery

Geotagged multispectral images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drone below:

  • Micasense RedEdge P Dual
    • two multispectral cameras operating side-by-side
    • 1.6 MP/band multispectral
    • 5.1 MP panchromatic
    • 10 spectral regions, including blue, green, red, red edge, near infrared
    • drones: Freefly AltaX, DJI Matrice 600 Pro

Collections include data from a downwelling radiation sensor and a ground calibration panel.

Further sensor details (external link)

Hyperspectral Imagery

Geotagged hyperspectral images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drone below:

  • Resonon Pika-L
    • 900-pixel line scanner
    • spectral region: 400-1000 nm

Collections include data from ground calibration panel.

Thermal Imagery

Geotagged thermal images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drones below:

  • FLIR Duo Pro R
    • 640 x 512 pixelsn (thermal)
    • Radiometric
    • 12 MP (RGB)
    • Drones: Freefly AltaX, DJI Matrice 600 Pro
  • Autel EVO II Pro
    • 640 x 512 pixels (thermal)
    • Non-radiometric
    • 48 MP (RGB)

LiDAR Point Cloud

Geotagged LiDAR point cloud can be collected and stored using LAS/LAZ or other formats. Point cloud acquisition possibilities include the sensor and drone below:

  • LiDAR USA Revolution 120
    • 300,000 Hz
    • 3 returns
    • Drone: Freefly AltaX
