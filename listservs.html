Information Sharing and Collaboration

Our center's listservs

The Center for Geospatial Research has three listsersvs:

CGR-ALL (cgr-all@listserv.uga.edu): A platform for announcements, collaborative discussions, and sharing insights with all individuals involved with CGR. Subscriber categories include students, management and research staff, postdocs and research scientists, CGR leadership, and affiliated researchers from groups outside CGR.

CGR-INT (cgr-int@listserv.uga.edu): Used for internal announcements, collaborative discussions, and sharing insights related to our work at the center. Subscriber categories include students, management and research staff, postdocs and research scientists, and CGR leadership.

CGR-LEAD (cgr-lead@listserv.uga.edu): Used for internal communication, collaborative discussions, and sharing insights among those leading efforts at CGR. Subscriber categories include management and research staff, postdocs and research scientists, as well as the center's director and associate directors.
