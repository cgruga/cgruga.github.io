RGB Sensor

Geotagged RGB images can be collected in compressed and/or raw format. Image acquisition possibilities include the cameras and drones below:

  • 20 MP camera 4/3 CMOS sensor (DJI Mavic 3)
  • 20 MP camera 4/3 CNOS sensor (DJI Mavic 3M, images can be collected simultaneous to multispectral data).
  • 20 MP camera 4/3 CMOS sensor (DJI Phantom 4 Pro)
  • 48 MP camera (Autel EVO II)

4-Band Multispectral Sensor

Geotagged multispectral images can be collected in lossless compressed format. Image acquisition possibilities include the camera and drone below:

  • 5 MP, spectral regions: green, red, red edge, near infrared; (DJI Mavic 3M, images can be collected simultaneous to 20 MP RGB data).

Collections include data from a downwelling radiation sensor and a ground calibration panel (optional)
