Graduate Research Assistant (PhD Level): GIS And Environmental Monitoring.

The Center for Geospatial Research and the Disruptive Geospatial Technologies Laboratory are seeking a PhD student to prepare and process environmental data collected as part of the monitoring program linked to the Savannah Harbor Expansion Project (SHEP). The graduate student will join the monitoring component of SHEP and will use GIS to prepare visualizations to be shared using the project's map portal.

Desired skills include familiarity with desktop Geographic Information Systems (ArcGIS Pro), the ability to ingest, transform and process multitemporal geospatial datasets collected by multiple sources and in multiple formats, and to use ArcGIS Online to populate an online map portal.

If interested, email cv to Dr. Sergio Bernardes (sbernard@uga.edu).

This is a one-year position renewable for three more years, based on performance and availability of funds.
